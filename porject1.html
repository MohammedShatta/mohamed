Design Approach

The design approach is one of understated elegance and thoughtful functionality, It emphasizes:
Clean Lines and Minimalism: Avoiding clutter and focusing on essential elements creates a sense of spaciousness.
Material Harmony: A cohesive selection of materials, primarily light wood tones, white, and subtle textures, ensures a harmonious flow between spaces.
Layered Lighting: Strategic placement of various light sources creates different moods and functionalities.
Textural Contrast: The combination of smooth surfaces (painted walls, cabinets) with rougher textures (macrame art, textured wall panel, rug) adds depth and interest.
Biophilic Integration: Bringing in elements like wood, plants, and natural light connects the interior with nature.

Color Palette

Primary Neutrals

Warm Whites/Off-Whites: Walls, ceilings, main sofa upholstery, kitchen cabinets, and various decorative elements create a bright and airy base.
Light Wood Tones: Found in flooring, TV unit, ceiling beams, and bedside tables, bringing warmth and natural texture.

Accent Colors

Forest Green/Emerald: A key accent color seen in the armchair in the living room, adding a pop of sophisticated color and connection to nature.
Terracotta / coral : A subtle accent on a throw pillow and a small side table, adding a touch of earthy warmth.
Black/Dark Grey: Used sparingly for TV screen, kitchen appliances, and track light fixtures, providing contrast and grounding the design.

Subtle Textural Hues

The textured wall panel in the living room introduces subtle variations of beige/grey, and the macrame art adds a soft, creamy texture.

Challenges & Solutions

Creating a Warm Feel in a Predominantly White/Neutral Space

Our Solution:

Extensive use of light-toned wood (flooring, TV unit, ceiling accents) and soft, inviting textiles (plush rug, soft sofa upholstery, sheer curtains) successfully introduces warmth and prevents the space from feeling cold.

Maximizing Storage without Clutter

Our Solution:

Utilizing built-in cabinetry (TV console, kitchen cabinets) and floating shelves provides ample storage that is integrated into the architectural design, maintaining clean lines.

Integrating a Large Television Seamlessly

Our Solution:

The TV is integrated into a custom-built, floating wooden wall unit, which includes open shelving, making it a cohesive part of the design rather than a standalone element.

Kitchen spacing and storage spaces

Our Solution:

we redesign the area and we make all cabinet to the celling making to opposite sides one for big appliance and one for stove and sink and keep the movement space suitable for streamlined move
